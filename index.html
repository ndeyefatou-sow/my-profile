🌍 Pictures Planet 📸

Last but certainly not least, we'll be taking a trip to the Pictures Planet. I must say we have some rocky history; at first I hated their favorite meals, cameras, and all who ate it, Photographers, the planet's inhabitant. I did not feel comfortable around them at all!
